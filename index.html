Review Yamaha R1M Motorcycle

Written by Adrian Pramana On June 26, 2021.

The 2021 Yamaha YZF-R1M is not much different from the 2021 Yamaha YZF-R1, but the specifications are higher. Compared to the previous generation, this bike has improved engine parts, reduced weight, and added various advanced features.

Yamaha YZF-R1M 2021 is equipped with an engine capacity of 998 cc. The engine is capable of producing maximum power of up to 200 PS at 13,500 rpm and maximum torque of up to 112.4 Nm at 11,500 rpm. The 2021 Yamaha YZF-R1M is also equipped with a variety of advanced features. The panel meter is fully digital and the lighting system uses LED technology. Yamaha YZF-R1M 2021 has a very sporty design. The body line also looks aggressive and gives off a slightly futuristic impression. Because the front is not like a full fairing sportbike in general.

Price Yamaha YZF-R1M: The 2021 Yamaha YZF-R1M is priced at IDR 812 million. This motor is offered with only one type. In terms of specifications, it is not much different from the 2021 Yamaha YZF-R1. This sport bike also uses a 4 stroke, inline 4 cylinders, DOHC, 4 valves, and liquid-cooled engine.
